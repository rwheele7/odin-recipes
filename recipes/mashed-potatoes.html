Easy Vegan Mashed Potatoes

Fluffy and buttery, easy to make mashed potatoes.

An easy side dish recipe that can be finished in less than 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Soy Milk
  • Non-Dairy Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Sage

Steps

  1. Peel and quarter the potatoes
  2. Put the potatoes in a large pot, then cover with water. Bring to a boil on high heat until the potatoes are soft
  3. Drain well and return potatoes to the pot
  4. Mash potatoes
  5. In a seperate pot, add soy milk, seasonings, and non-dairy butter. Heat until melted.
  6. Add the butter sauce to the potatoes and stir until mixed throughly.
  7. Serve!
